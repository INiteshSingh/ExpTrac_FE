The Motivation

Me being a Aspiring Software Engineer also own a small Personal Business and Tracking and Managing the Purchases is a Very Daunting and a Time Consuming task.

In the Start My Business was easy to track and keep track of stuff like sales and stock purchase. But as my Business Grew I Started to Forget things like how much and from where i bought a particular item for my Business

This Became my initial Motivation for Creating this tool and after that i wanted to help other small business owners like myself out there.

The Mission

In today’s world, money is one of the most important assets — but having control over your hard-earned money is an even more powerful. My mission is to help you take charge of your finances by making expense tracking simple, clear, and empowering. Whether you're saving for a goal, cutting unnecessary costs, or just trying to understand where your money goes, this tool is designed to support you every step of the way. Because when you have control over your money, you gain control over your future .

How Acer Works?

Track your daily expenses, categorize your spending, and view insightful summaries to make smarter financial decisions. Designed to be user-friendly and accessible, whether you’re just starting out or already budgeting like a pro.

You Can Track things as simple as What did i buy ?, from where did i buy that thing ?, how much did that thing cost me ? to things like What % of money i spent on food, clothing, travel etc.
